Bosonic classical-into-quantum code

Description

Bosonic code designed for transmission of classical information through non-classical channels. Typically, such codes encode real numbers into coherent states for transmission over a quantum channel and decoding with a quantum receiver.

Rate

The Holevo capacity has been calculated for various bosonic quantum channels such as AGWN [1] (see Ref. [2] for a review).

Cousins

  • Bosonic code — Bosonic classical-into-quantum codes are bosonic codes designed to transmit classical information.
  • Coherent-state constellation quantum code — Typical bosonic classical-into-quantum codes encode classical alphabets into constellations of coherent states.
  • Analog code — Bosonic classical-into-quantum codes are extensions of analog codes to transmission of classical information over quantum analog channels.
