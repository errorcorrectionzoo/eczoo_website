Name
Oscillator-into-oscillator code[1][2] 
Description
Encodes Hilbert space of \(\ell^2\)-normalizable functions on \(\mathbb{R}^k\) into that on \(\mathbb{R}^n\). Usually denoted as \(((n,k))_{\mathbb{R}}\).
