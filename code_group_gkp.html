Group GKP codes

Name
Group GKP codes §
Description
Encodes finite-dimensional Hilbert space into the Hilbert space of \(\ell^2\)-normalizable functions on a group \(G\). Construction is based on nested subgroups \(H\subset K \subset G\), where \(H,K\) are finite. The \(|K|/|H|\)-dimensional logical subspace is spanned by basis states that are equal superpositions of elements of cosets of \(H\) in \(K\).
Protection
Protects against generalized bit-flip errors \(g\in G\) that are inside the fundamental domain of \(G/K\). Protection against phase-flip errors determined by branching rules of irreps of \(G\) into those of \(K\), and further into those of \(H\).
